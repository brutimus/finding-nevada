With the approaching 150th birthday of Nevada's statehood, the Sun has dispatched Matt Hufman and Mike Smith from the newsroom to explore, define and explain our state. Over the course of seven extensive road trips, they will be writing, taking photos and videos and sketching the fascinating people and places -- obvious and obscure -- that distinguish the Silver State. Join them in finding Nevada.
